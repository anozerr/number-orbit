Representative winning paths for each star band. Easy levels always award 3 stars on completion; Medium/Hard levels list verified 3/2/1-star paths from the actual orbit items.

Levels 16-45 are checked for shortcuts and for full star-band reachability: the shortest possible completion always equals the intended 3-star length (no accidental faster win exists), and a verified path exists at the exact 3-star, 2-star and 1-star move counts for every one of these 30 levels. 17 of the 30 keep at least one operator greyed out at the very start; the rest are fully tappable from move one. Because a few levels only stayed shortcut-free by keeping (or regaining) a grey-at-start operator, the original "no two consecutive levels grey at start" spread is no longer exact.
